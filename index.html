Castle In The Sky

天空の城ラピュタ

An enchanting tale of the battle between technology and nature.

By Visionary Director Hayao Miyazaki

Fun Facts about Castle In The Sky:

  • Synopsis - Pazu and Sheeta set out to find Laputa, a castle floating in the sky. They begin their adventure to find Laputa and run into many trying to stop them.
  • Laputa was inspired by Paronella Park, a castle built by Jose Paronella at Mena Creek, in Far North Queensland, Australia. The theme music from "Castle in the Sky" is used during the night tours of the castle.
  • The Kingdom of Laputariches, or "Laputa", was built when a great technological civilization over 700 years ago (the one in the story's present-time is the second, as depicted by the film's opening) thrived, by a people who fled to the sky out of hatred for the wars of the Earth.[2]
